Instructor

I am a fully qualified female driving instructor who is patient, friendly and very supportive. I passed all the qualifying exams to be an Approved Driving Instructor on my first attempt. I hold a *green ADI badge, which only fully qualified instructors carry. I offer a structured lesson plan to ensure pupils receive the correct level of training for every stage. The lessons are tailored to suit each individual, to ensure they are both effective and enjoyable. My aim is to provide high quality driving tuition, in a safe and pleasant training environment.

Vehicles

I have two high specification Vauxhall Corsa Cars. Both offer comfort and safety to ensure you have an enjoyable driving experience on your lessons. The specification includes air conditioning, fully adjustable seats, electric mirrors, dual controls and numerous other safety features. The Vauxhall Corsa has won two accolades: What Car Magazine – Car Of The Year 2007 and Training Car Of The Year 2006. What better recommendations do you need?
