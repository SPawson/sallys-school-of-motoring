Instructor

Sally is a fully qualified female driving instructor with over 10 years’ experience. She has passed all the qualifying exams to be an Approved Driving Instructor on her first attempt. She holds a *green ADI badge, which only fully qualified instructors carry and offers a structured lesson plan to ensure pupils receive the correct level of training for every stage. The lessons are tailored to suit each individual, to ensure they are both effective and enjoyable. Sally is patient, friendly and very supportive towards all her students and aims to provide high quality driving tuition, in a safe and pleasant training environment.

Vehicles

Sally uses two high specification Vauxhall Corsa Cars, both of which offer comfort and safety to ensure you have an enjoyable driving experience on your lessons. The specification includes air conditioning, fully adjustable seats, electric mirrors, dual controls and numerous other safety features. The Vauxhall Corsa has won numerous awards and accolades over its many years, which makes it an excellent car to learn to drive in.

Other Services

Fleet Assesor

I provide assessments for full license holders, in which I offer guidance, support and advice for drivers to enhance their safety on the road. I work with both medium and large corporations to ensure employees are driving both company and private cars in a safe and controlled manner.

Internal Quality Assurance

I hold an IQA qualification in which I work for independent companies to observe and judge the evidence of an assessor’s portfolio to ensure they have met the correct criteria when testing and coaching full license holders. I make informed judgements on the practice of and decisions of the assessment team to maintain high standards of testing and training.

Assesor Qualification

This qualification allows me to deliver apprenticeships, including government funded schemes, as well as BTEC driver training and other courses. I also hold an OTL qualification in which I observe other assessors and trainers whilst they deliver training to learners to ensure a high level of teaching is being provided.
